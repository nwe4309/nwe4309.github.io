About Me

Hello! I'm Nick Engell! I have over eight years of programming experience and I graduated from RIT with a Bachelors and Masters in Game Design & Development in 2022. There I spent a lot of time developing and honing my development skills as well as my design skills. I've always had a strong passion for programming and creating something that I could see and interact with. Gameplay programming and Web development are the two applications of my skills that I found to utilize my passion the most. Being able to program a feature and then see it work and appear before my eyes is a wonderful thing.
