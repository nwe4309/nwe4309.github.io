VS Snowman

Development (January 2020)

School Project

Description:

• VS Snowman is a 3D first person snowman shooter created in Unity using C#.
• Developed in one week with collaboration from Japanese students of KCG (Kyoto Computer Gakuin).
• The team was composed of six members, three from RIT and three from KCG. I was the lead programmer and oversaw creating the base scripts and classes for the player and enemy. As well, I setup movement, created the ability to shoot snowballs, dig up more ammo, and created the enemy AI.
• Because of the massive time zone diffence between RIT and KCG, we decided to split up the work based on location
• The RIT members primarily focused on the development of the game with some UI and graphical changes while the KCG members primarily focused on creating art and music assets, as well as the overall game design of the game.
