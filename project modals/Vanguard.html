Cardfight Vanguard Simulator

Development (March 2018 - November 2019)

Passion Project

Description:

• Cardfight! Vanguard Simulator is my attempt at recreating the physical card game Cardfight! Vanguard in a virtual space.
• From seeing all the fan made simulators for Yu-gi-oh, Pokemon, and Magic the Gathering, it gave me hope there was one for vanguard. However, I was disappointed to find that the only ones out there were poorly made and not what I was looking for. Therefore, I took it upon myself to create a simulator for it myself.
• I am the sole programmer and all card images belong to Bushiroad.
• Project was created in Unity using C# and unfortunately discontinued part way through.
• This project is about 35% complete and had to be stopped as school work and other projects at the time severely limited the amount of time I could commit.
