Seeking Solanum

Development (October 2018)

Passion Project

Description:

• Seeking Solanum is a short horror game made by developers for FearJam in Unity using C#.
• We had 48 hours to make a horror game with the themes of fear of loss, unknown, and or consequence.
• In the game, you find yourself in a cabin with notes scattered around. As you read the notes you learn of the story of the cabin. As well, you also hear a man at the door.
• Listening to his dialog you learn why you're in the cabin and soon learn what to fear.
• My role in the game was building the outside terrain, creating the tunnel, the cabin walls, the coding of dialog timing, the inspect object script, the monster appearing
at the right time in the cabin and tunnel, and the triggering of the trap door.
