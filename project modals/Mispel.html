Mispel

Development (November 2019 - May 2022)

Passion Project

Description:

• Mispel brings 2D platform-fighter combat to a single player adventure. Explore uncharted lands, meet friends and foes, and take their powers for yourself. Swap between movesets on the fly to craft your own unique combos and playstyle.
• The goal of the game is to create a compelling single player campaign which Super Smash Bros. lacks.
• The current build here is a complete demo of the first level with a fully functional boss at the end as well as a small section of a second level.
• Mispel was created in Unity using C# with a team of four.
• My role was the lead programmer and I was in charge of coding most of the major systems.
• A few of the notable systems I created includes the combat, the movement, and the AI of the boss and enemies.
